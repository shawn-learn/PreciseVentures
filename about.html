About Precise Ventures

Our Vision

Precise Ventures is dedicated to enhancing personal wellness through technology. We believe that data-driven insights empower individuals to achieve peak performance and long-term health.

Meet the Team

Shawn Learn – Founder & Chief Technology Officer

Shawn Learn blends expertise in kinesiology, engineering, and software systems gained from over two decades in mission‑critical roles. With degrees in both fields, he has studied human performance and built rigorous real-time platforms. For more than 18 years, Shawn specialized in implementing business logic into a decision support system for pipeline operations. He also holds the Certified Strength and Conditioning Specialist (CSCS) credential. At Precise Ventures he channels that interdisciplinary background into reliable fitness‑technology solutions that help users train smarter and safer.

How We Operate

Organizer follows a freemium approach so anyone can start tracking workouts for free. Individuals may subscribe to remove ads and unlock advanced analytics, while coaches and teams can choose tiers that include client management and integrations. Enterprises and clinics have licensing options tailored to their needs.

We also collaborate with wellness partners and offer optional content packs, keeping the core experience accessible and the platform sustainable.
